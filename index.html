Greetings everyone!
My name is Asier
If you didn’t already know,
my name means “the beginning”
and I am here to guide you
through many different worlds.
Each artwork in this exhibition
began with a single object.
And each object contains a world.
As you visit these worlds, I want you to think
, to reimagine, to be concerned about
your relationship with your world
and its mundane objects.
Let’s begin this expedition!
